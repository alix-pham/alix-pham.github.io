> [Contact me](/contact)

Originally from **Toulouse, France**, I currently live in **Geneva, Switzerland**.

I design programs, convenings, and experimental formats for impact-focused communities — currently as **Programs & Initiatives Lead at [Effective Altruism France](https://altruismeefficacefrance.org/)**, and through freelance and grant-funded work with biosecurity communities.

With a background in biomedical engineering, I have worked across **biosecurity**, **AI governance**, and **community building**, previously at the [Simon Institute for Longterm Governance](https://www.simoninstitute.ch/) (via the Talos Fellowship) and as Co-Director of **[Effective Altruism Switzerland](https://effectivealtruism.ch/)**.

#### Work & projects:
- **[Effective Altruism France](https://altruismeefficacefrance.org/)** — Programs & Initiatives Lead; designing programs, events, and community-building initiatives
- **Biosecurity** — grant-funded work for field building

#### I am interested in:
- **program & convening design:** crafting formats that generate real learning and connection
- **community building:** creating conditions for people to do their best work together
- **biosecurity & biosafety:** managing risks from biological agents and their products
- **AI governance:** ensuring advanced AI development goes well for humanity
- **resilient foods:** food production methods that hold up under global catastrophic shocks
