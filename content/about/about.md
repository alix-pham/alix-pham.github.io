Originally from **Toulouse, France**, I currently live in **Geneva, Switzerland**.

I design programs, convenings, and experimental formats for impact-focused communities — currently as **Programs & Initiatives Lead at [Effective Altruism France](https://altruismeefficacefrance.org/)**, and through freelance and grant-funded work with biosecurity communities.

With a background in biomedical engineering, I have worked across **biosecurity**, **AI governance**, and **community building**, previously at the [Simon Institute for Longterm Governance](https://www.simoninstitute.ch/) (via the Talos Fellowship) and as Co-Director of **Effective Altruism Switzerland**.

A generalist who gravitates toward people-oriented work, I have built a wide network across the impact ecosystem. I am happy to share learnings on non-linear career paths, weighing fit across different fields, and program and convening design.
